# Dart Penalty Playback

An open-source, offline-first Android darts scorer built with Unity that brings personality, custom voice announcements, and funny penalties to your game night.

The source code is publicly available, while this repository serves primarily as an **issue tracker**, **download hub**, and host for the **Privacy Policy**.

The app is completely **ad-free**, **tracker-free**, and works entirely **offline**.

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 Multiple Game Modes

Play your favorite darts formats with extensive customization options.

Supported modes:

- **X01**
- **Cricket**
- **Around the Clock**

Configure matches exactly how you like them, including:

- Adjustable **Sets & Legs**
- Various mode-specific settings
- Single-player, local multiplayer, or matches against AI bots


### 🤖 AI Opponents

Practice anytime against computer-controlled players.

Features include:

- Multiple difficulty levels
- Casual training sessions or competitive matches
- Great for solo practice without needing another player


### 👥 Local Player Profiles

Create and manage player profiles directly on your device.

Features include:

- Fully offline accounts
- Persistent player data
- Individual statistics and history tracking


### 📊 Detailed Statistics

Track your progress over time with comprehensive statistics for all supported game modes.

Available periods:

- Today
- This Week
- This Month
- This Year
- Lifetime Statistics

Analyze your performance, compare sessions, and see how your game evolves.


### 🗂️ Match History

Every saved match contributes to your personal history.

Features include:

- Browse previously played matches
- Edit or remove stored entries
- Correct mistakes retrospectively
- Keep long-term records of your dart sessions


### 💸 Custom Penalty System

Turn mistakes into entertainment.

Create your own penalty rules and define custom costs for events such as:

- Wall hits
- Ceiling hits
- Missing with every dart
- Hitting lucky numbers (e. g. 111, 222 etc.)
- Hitting ones three times in a row
- Losing a match

Perfect for adding some extra motivation—or chaos—to game night.


### 🎙️ Voice Recorder & Custom Sounds

Give the app its own personality.

Features include:

- Built-in recorder for creating custom voice clips
- Record announcements directly inside the app
- Assign clips to specific events such as:
  - Scores
  - Penalties
  - Match events
  - Milestones
- Choose between the app's default sound pack and your own recordings at any time


### 🎨 Personalization

Customize the look of the app to match your setup.

Features include:

- **6 integrated color themes**
- Easy switching between themes
- Consistent styling across the entire app
- Optimized for dark mode


### 🔒 Privacy First

Your data stays on your device.

Dart Penalty Playback:

- Requires no internet connection
- Contains no advertisements
- Uses no analytics services
- Includes no trackers
- Stores all data locally in JSON files
- Keeps audio recordings exclusively on your phone

No accounts, no cloud, no hidden data collection.
